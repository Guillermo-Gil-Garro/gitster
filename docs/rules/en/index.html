WHAT IS THIS? 🪩 ▾

GITSTER is a spin on the famous put-the-songs-in-chronological-order game, but here the tokens set the beat way more 🕺
In this version, bets and card steals aren't just forgettable featurings, they're the headliner! 💃

WHAT DO YOU NEED TO PLAY? 🧰 ▾
  • The base game (cards + tokens) 🧩
  • A phone with the apps (HGitster and Spotify) 🎶
  • A speaker (optional but recommended) 🔊
1) GOAL 🎯 ▾

Build your Timeline by placing songs in CHRONOLOGICAL ORDER (before/after) ⌛

Whoever ends the game with 12 cards in their Timeline wins 🏁

(The Exact Year is NOT needed to place cards; it's only good for winning tokens)

2) SETUP 📋 ▾
  • Place the deck in the middle
    Each player starts with 1 starting card in their Timeline (face up) 🃏
  • Who's the first DJ? 🎧🎛️
    (The DJ is the player the song is playing for)
    Look at the year on your starting Timeline card
    The player with the OLDEST song goes first 📻

TIE – Tied players draw an extra card (it doesn't count for the Timeline, it gets discarded)
Whoever gets the MOST RECENT song wins ✨

  • Every player receives
    3 betting tokens 🪙🪙🪙

TOKEN LIMIT
YOU CANNOT HOARD MORE THAN 10 TOKENS! ⚠️
You'll soon see that in GITSTER you rack up tokens way faster than usual, so dare to bet and let your inner gambling addict run wild! 🤑

3) DJ'S TURN 🎤 ▾

The DJ takes a card from the deck (face down), scans the song and decides what to do:

  •  PLAY IT NORMAL 🎵
    Listen to the song, place it
    FACE DOWN in the Timeline, 🕒
    and try to guess the Title, Artist(s) and Exact Year 🎵🎤📆

RHYTHM – You can listen to the song UP TO THE CHORUS (included), no listening to the whole thing ⏱️
If there's no chorus, max
1 minute ⏳

  •  RE-ROLL 🎲
    The DJ can pay tokens to discard the scanned card and take a new card from the deck 🤞

    • Cost per turn (cumulative):
    the first re-roll of the turn costs
    1 token, the second 2, the third 3;
    and so on 📈

  •  BUY THE CARD 💸
    By paying 5 tokens, the DJ can flip the card over and place it in the right spot of their Timeline 🧐

    • On that turn, there will be NO
    BETTING ROUND 🚫

LAW OF ADVANTAGE – The player(s) with the most cards CANNOT BUY CARDS (but they CAN RE-ROLL or EXPROPRIATE A CARD) ⚖️

  •  EXPROPRIATE A CARD 🫳
    By paying 10 tokens, the DJ can discard the scanned card and steal a card from another player's Timeline (placing it in the right spot of their own Timeline) 🦹‍♂️

    • CAREFUL! Spending 10 tokens leaves you at 0, so you can't bet and, therefore, it'll take you at least one round to start winning tokens again 😱

    On that turn, there will be NO
    BETTING ROUND 🚫

4) BETTING ROUND
🎰🎰🎰🎰🎰🎰🎰🎰🎰🎰 ▾

No more fighting over who said GITSTER first, who shouted the loudest, who gets to be goalkeeper because the ball is theirs and if we don't let them they'll go home and take it with them and blah, blah, blah!

Here we're gambling addicts civilized players and, as such, we follow an order 🫡

The betting round starts with the player to the DJ's right ➡️

Going COUNTERCLOCKWISE (to the right), players can decide to:

  • BET 1 token on ONLY ONE of the following categories:
    Chronological Order, Title, Artist(s) OR Exact Year 🕒🎵🎤📆

NO REPEATS – If a player before you (DJ included) has already bet on a Timeline Zone (Chronological Order) you wanted to bet on, or has said a Title, Artist(s) or Exact Year you wanted to bet on; you pass or bet on something else 🤷‍♂️

In short: TOUGH LUCK, KEEP DANCING (quite literally) 🕺

VARIOUS ARTISTS – When betting on Artist(s), you can name one or several (to steal token-winning chances from the players who go after you) 🖕; but if you name several artists AND GET AT LEAST ONE WRONG, YOU LOSE THE BET ☠️

If you get them ALL right, you collect ONLY ONCE:

  • +1 if you only named
    main Artist(s)
  • +2 if you named at least one
    NON-main Artist (feat)

(see Bet Payouts)

LAW OF ADVANTAGE II, this time it's personal – The player(s) with the most cards ALSO CANNOT BET ON CHRONOLOGICAL ORDER, but they CAN SCREW THE DJ ⚖️

  • SCREW THE DJ, paying 3 tokens so the DJ CANNOT win that card 😈
    • If the DJ gets it right, the card is discarded
    • If the DJ gets it wrong, it's resolved as normal
      (a player who bet on Chronological Order can take it)

    The screwing is decided when betting, but it's executed after revealing and after collecting/paying

  • PASS 🤷‍♀️
5) TURN RESOLUTION AND BET PAYOUTS 💵 ▾

Once every non-DJ player has bet, the card can be flipped over:

  • If the DJ placed it right in their Timeline, they keep it 👍 (unless someone Screwed them, in which case it's discarded)
  • Otherwise, it's discarded, unless someone bet on Chronological Order and got it right, in which case they place it in the right spot of their own Timeline 🎉

Next, following the betting order (and starting with the DJ), the corresponding tokens are collected/paid:

  • For the DJ, as long as they placed the card right in their Timeline (otherwise, they collect NOTHING):
    • Title: +1 🙂
    • Artist(s) (doesn't matter if one or several, main or not): +1 😊
    • Exact Year: +1 😄
    • They can win at most
      +3 tokens 😁🤩🥳
  • For the rest of the players (bets against the house):
    • Title: +1 🙂
    • Main Artist: +1 😊
    • NON-main Artist: +2 😄😁
    • Exact Year: +2 🤩🥳
    • If you lost your bet, you leave
      1 token in the box (-1) 😭
    • If you passed (didn't bet), you're a chicken and you stay as you are 💩

TITLE-ITIS – For a Title bet to count, you have to say it (almost) PERFECTLY, with its articles, its tiny little words and all ☝️🤓
The only thing you're allowed to skip are the "subtitles" in parentheses
E.g.: For Blue (Da Ba Dee),
by Eiffel 65, "Blue" is enough
(but who on earth says just "Blue" for Blue (Da Ba Dee)?!)

MAIN ARTIST OR FEAT?
A song can have one or several main artists (E.g.: the Bizarrap Sessions)
“NON-main artist” = guest artist, feat, extra, etc. (however you interpret it at the table, I wash my hands of it) 🙈🙉🙊

REMEMBER THE MAXIMUM LIMIT OF 10 TOKENS!
6) END OF THE GAME 🏁 ▾

When someone reaches 12 cards in their Timeline, the game does NOT end 🙅‍♂️

  • It's checked and ends at the end of the round, when the turn comes back to the player who was the first DJ ↩️
  • If the leader is at 12, 13, or 14 cards, they also need a lead of
    ≥ 2 cards over the next player with the most cards
    (12–10, 13–11 or 14–12) 🎾
  • If at the end of the round someone has reached 15 cards, they win NO MATTER WHAT 🏆
    (unless there's a tie at 15)

TIE AT 15 – Whoever has the most tokens wins 🪙🪙🪙🪙🪙🪙🪙🪙

TOKEN TIE 😵‍💫 – If the tie continues: SUDDEN DEATH ☠️
The tied players play 1 turn each (in the normal order) and the first one to get +1 card (WITHOUT BUYING) wins 👑
If both get it in the same round, whoever ends with the most tokens wins 🥇

CHEATSHEET 🧠 ▾
  • Whoever has the oldest starting Timeline song goes first 📻
  • In bets, the player to the DJ's right always goes first ➡️
  • Starting tokens: 3 🪙🪙🪙
  • Token limit: 10 ⚠️
  • You can listen to the song up to the chorus (included)
    If there's no chorus: 1 minute ⏳
  • Re-roll: 1st costs 1, 2nd costs 2, 3rd costs 3… (in the same turn) 🎲
  • Buy a card: costs 5 tokens AND ONLY IF YOU'RE NOT IN THE LEAD 💸
  • Bets in order and COUNTERCLOCKWISE (to the right): 1 token, 1 category, and no repeating picks 🚫
    If you're winning or tied for the lead, you CANNOT BET on Chronological Order ⚖️
  • SCREW THE DJ: instead of betting, pay 3 tokens so the DJ CANNOT win that card 😈
    If the DJ gets the Timeline placement right, it's discarded
    If the DJ gets it wrong and someone bet on Chronological Order, they can take it
  • EXPROPRIATE: on your turn, pay 10 tokens (you drop to 0), discard the scanned card and steal a card from another player's Timeline 🫳
  • If you get (at least) one artist wrong, you lose the Artist(s) bet ☠️
  • The DJ role rotates to the right when a turn ends ➡️
  • You win by reaching 12–14 cards in the Timeline with a lead ≥ 2,
    (at 15 cards you win NO MATTER WHAT)
    AT THE END OF THE ROUND ↩️

How many players is it for? Can you play in teams?

GITSTER is a game designed for 4–7 players 🚶‍♂️🚶‍♀️🚶🚶‍♂️+🚶‍♀️🚶🚶‍♂️

But relax, Mister Sociable 😎 / Miss Popularity 👸, if you are
8 or more players, you can play
in teams, though you'll have to agree on the Timeline and Betting decisions together 🤝

  • 8 players = 4 pairs 🧑‍🤝‍🧑🧑‍🤝‍🧑🧑‍🤝‍🧑🧑‍🤝‍🧑
  • 9 players = 3 trios 👪👪👪 or 3 pairs + 1 trio 🧑‍🤝‍🧑🧑‍🤝‍🧑🧑‍🤝‍🧑👪
  • 10 players = 5 pairs 🧑‍🤝‍🧑🧑‍🤝‍🧑🧑‍🤝‍🧑🧑‍🤝‍🧑🧑‍🤝‍🧑
  • 11 players = 3 trios + 1 pair 👪👪👪🧑‍🤝‍🧑
  • 12 players = 4 trios 👪👪👪👪

And we won't give you more examples because we think you've got it (and because we know nobody has more than 12 friends, don't flatter yourselves)

📜 Why do the “Laws of Advantage” exist? (why nerf the player who's winning?)

Most of the game's additional rules (the ones in fuchsia and blue boxes) are designed to balance the game and prevent exploits ⚙️

  • For example, the player(s) in the lead can't buy cards to prevent a SNOWBALL effect (the ones winning pulling even further ahead of the ones losing) 🦾⛄️
  • Likewise, the leader(s) can't bet on chronological order because it's a simple fix for the fact that, with a "bad DJ", the player to their right always gets betting priority and could steal lots of cards betting on Chronological Order, which would give them too big an edge over the rest of the table just for knowing where to sit 💪💪

In the end it's about giving everyone equal opportunities to make the game more competitive and, therefore, more fun 🤪

🎧 Can you rewind or replay the song?

No. One single playback per turn, no going back 🙅‍♂️⏪

🗣️ Can you talk during the listening or give hints?

You can talk and trash-talk, but no direct hints 🤫
You can NOT shout to screw people over and keep them from hearing the song 🤐

💸 Buying the card (5 tokens)

When can I buy it? Whenever you want (no need to wait for the chorus) ⚡️

🤔 If I buy, can I win tokens for Title/Artist/Exact Year?

No: if you buy, you collect no tokens that turn 🙅‍♀️

🎲 Can I re-roll even if I've already listened to the song?

Yes, but try to keep the game moving 🥱

If you can tell you have no clue about the song from the start, re-roll fast, spending a token is no big deal (and I doubt Our Lady of the Remix will suddenly appear to you and reveal what year the song is from) 😂

⚔️ Disputes (“almost perfect” title, main vs feat, etc.)

Decided by a quick vote by show of hands
(majority rules) 🙋‍♀️🗳️🙋‍♂️

If there's a tie: it counts as valid, but you collect +1 (even if it would normally be +2) 🤷‍♂️

⏱️ What if the song has no clear chorus?

You listen for a max of 1 minute (someday the app will cut it off automatically) ✋

🔟 What if I'm at 10 tokens and I'm owed more?

You stay at 10 🥲
The excess is lost 🙏
In general, when in doubt, remember: TOUGH LUCK, KEEP DANCING 💃
